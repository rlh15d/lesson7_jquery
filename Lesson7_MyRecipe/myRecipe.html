Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 (16 ounce) can pumpkin
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 1 and 1/4 cups vegetable
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 t baking soda
  • 2 t baking powder
  • 2 t cinnamon
  • 1 t salt
  • 12 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips (regular or mini)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.

In large mixing bowl beat eggs, sugar, pumpkin, vanilla, and oil until smooth.

Mix dry ingredients together in a separate bowl.

Fold dry ingredients into pumpkin mixture.

Fold in chocolate chips.

Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups 3/4 full.

Bake at 400 fahrenheit for 16-20 minutes.

Let cool before taking out of pan.
